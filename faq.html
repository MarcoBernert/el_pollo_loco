EL Pollo Loco Experience

Answers to Frequently Asked Questions

What's the key to success in the game?

Your mission: stay alive and conquer the final challenge. Gather coins, trade them for bottles, defeat foes, and prepare to confront the ultimate adversary for victory!

How can I maneuver the character?

Navigate your character left and right with the arrow keys. Press space or D to jump. Deploy bottles with "D" and purchase them with your collected coins with "F".

How do I destroy adversaries?

There are two methods: jump on them or throw bottles. With enough bottles, even the final boss can be defeated!

What occurs if the character is harmed?

If attacked by chickens or chicks, your character loses a life. Stay vigilant, evade or eliminate threats before they strike, especially the final boss!

How can I monitor the character's status?

Displayed are five bars:
  • Character's Health
  • Bottles Inventory
  • Coins Collected
  • Final Boss's Health

What's the purpose of coins?

Coins hold value! Exchange 1 coin for a bottle, essential for battling enemies or defeating the final boss.

How can I reset the game?

Restart by reloading the page or using the in-game restart button.

How do I activate fullscreen mode?

Click the fullscreen icon; exit with the "Esc" key. For convenience, exit fullscreen before restarting.

How do I toggle sound?

Click the audio icon. If in fullscreen, exit fullscreen for sound controls.
